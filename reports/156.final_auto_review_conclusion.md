2. No single seed dominates; selection must be per-sample.
3. Direct q0/g0 interventions are brittle.
4. Real-pair AV offset scorers do not automatically transfer to generated-candidate ranking.
5. The open problem is generated-domain candidate scoring.

Only one genuinely different direction remains:

> Train a generated-domain candidate ranker using non-final-metric supervision.
